Scorpion API

scorpionapi.com/api/v1/scorpions

Get 15 scorpions

scorpionapi.com/api/v1/scorpions/id

*id will be equal to a number from 1 through 10

example: scorpionapi.com/api/v1/scorpions/5 returns a scorpion with id of 5

Get a list of posionous scorpions from the 10

scorpionapi.com/api/v1/scorpions/range/num

*num will be equal to a number from 1 through 15

example: scorpionapi.com/api/v1/scorpions/range/7 returns list of 7 scorpions

Get a specific number of scorpions (1 through 10 will be valid numbers)

scorpionapi.com/api/v1/scorpions/poisonous

Get a list of posionous scorpions
